St John's Computer Game Design and Development

Welcome

St John's Game Design and Development is a hands-on, practical course.

In first year, you'll focus on 2D games, creating backgrounds, animated characters and user interfaces, which you’ll use to create games of your own design.

In second year, you'll make 3D games, using your own 3D environments and animated characters.

You’ll finish the course with a solid grounding in programming, asset creation and game design, and with an impressive portfolio of projects. Graduates often continue their studies in one of several linked courses in CIT, UCC and LIT.
